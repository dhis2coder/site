About Us

We are a team of experienced Monitoring and Evaluation (M&E) specialists, data analysts, and DHIS2 experts with over 10 years of combined experience in designing, configuring, and deploying scalable health data management systems. Our expertise spans DHIS2 tracker program development, Data Visualizations, and capacity building, with a focus on HIV/TB and other health-related projects. With advanced degrees in Public Health and Monitoring & Evaluation, we have successfully supported national programs and international organizations like World Vision (IRS), ABT Associates, and PSI. Our commitment lies in delivering innovative solutions tailored to improve data-driven decision-making and program outcomes.
